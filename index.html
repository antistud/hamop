Building unity among Christ's followers

OUR
PURPOSE

      Promoting Spiritual growth among men of the Hastings Area so they become godly leaders in their Homes, Churches, and Community.

 

  • We believe

    the Bible to be the inspired, the only infallible, authoritative Word of God.

    We believe

    that there is one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son and Holy Spirit.

    We believe

    in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ, in His virgin birth, in His sinless life, in His miracles, in His substitutionary and aton- ing death through His blood, in His bodily resurrection, in His ascension to the right hand of the Father, and in His personal return in power and glory.

  • We believe

    that for the salvation of lost and sinful people, regeneration by the Holy Spirit is absolutely essential.

    We believe

    in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a godly life.

    We believe

    in the resurrection of both the saved and the lost; they that are saved unto the resurrection of life and they that are lost unto the resurrection of damnation.

    We believe

    in the spiritual unity of believers in our Lord Jesus Christ.

WHO
WE
ARE

      We are repentant sinners saved by the Grace of God through our total faith and trust in our Lord and Savior JESUS CHRIST.

      We are a band of Brothers from various congregations who love JESUS with all our hearts, who have banded together for the common purpose of making JESUS famous in this area through love.

      We are men who are willing to reach beyond racial and denominational barriers to demonstrate the power of biblical unity.
(John 17:20-23)

 

HAMOP members promote unity within the body of Christ without regard to denominational or racial differences, develop godly men who will seek to keep the Great Commandment and who are committed to the fulfillment of the Great Commission while providing assistance to other Christian ministries.

WHAT
WE
DO

1. We honor JESUS through worship, prayer and obedience to God’s Word in the power of the Holy Spirit.
2. We pursue vital relationships with other men, understanding that we need brothers to help each other be accountable.
3. We support the mission of His Church by honoring and praying for our Pastors, and actively giving of our time and resources.
4. We practice spiritual, moral, ethical, and sexual purity, while building strong marriages and families through love, protection and biblical values.
5. We plan and host events and gatherings that expands God’s Kingdom here in Hastings and the surrounding area.
6. We meet the 1st Wednesday each month from 12:00 noon to 1:00pm at 835 S. Burlington, conference room for lunch and fellowship.
